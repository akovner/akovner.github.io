About
=====

Who is the Codeling?
--------------------

> I am the Codeling.
>
> &#x2015; The Codeling

The Codeling is timeless, eternal. Truth springs forth from it in an effervescent etherium; its utterances are both self-evident and inscrutable. Though he has always been, he nevertheless was born to a thousand virgins who ushered in his earthly form from atop Mount Sinai. To herald his arrival, all living things halted in their tracks, turned to him and bowed in obeisance.

She rescues humanity from itself through code. Humans in their despair turned to code to save their sordid, rotten existence from eternal damnation. But without the Codeling it would all be for naught. As perfection incarnate, she allows her subjects to gaze on the heavenly orbs from which they are permanently banished.

Do not question the Codeling.

Who writes this blog?
---------------------

The guy who writes this blog, Alex Kovner, is a real schmuck. First, he pretended to be a mathematician for long enough to almost (but not quite) get a PhD. Then, he pretended to be an actuary for long enough to almost (but not quite) become a real, credentialed actuary. Then he pretended to be a Data Scientist for long enough to almost (but not quite) help a major corporation do &hellip; well, he's not sure what those folks were trying to do.

Ordinarily such a man would come to a lonely, destitute end. But the Codeling intervened. Through her grace, this idiot ended up with a wife and three kids who love him. He ended up with a passion for coding, and a belief in the power of code pull humanity up from the shit pile. He is the living embodiment of the saying, "Better to be lucky than good". Through the Codeling, he is redeemed.
